About the Project

Drowsy driving is one of the leading causes of vehicular-related accidents, accounting for more than 1,200 fatal crashes and roughly 90,000 police‑reported accidents in the United States each year. Commercial and passenger vehicles are lacking in an affordable, reliable means of accurately detecting drowsiness before it turns fatal.

Awake‑Pilot aims to address this problem by providing real-time alerts to the user once signs of fatigue are detected. Upon starting the vehicle, the dashboard-mounted device continuously monitors for changes in the driver’s face and upper body for any indicators of drowsiness in order to issue an alert to the driver if needed.
