The Awake-Pilot System is an advanced driver-monitoring solution that integrates hardware and software components to detect driver fatigue and prevent accidents caused by drowsy driving. The system utilizes data from radar sensors and cameras, processed through a Jetson Nano, to activate an alert system designed to wake the user through sound and vibration.

Image Processing Subsystem (Blue):

  • Facial Movement Indicators: Detects specific facial cues such as eye closure and head tilt to assess driver fatigue.
  • Logitech C270 Camera: Captures real-time video feed of the driver's face.
  • OpenCV Fatigue Detection: Processes facial data to identify signs of drowsiness using AI-driven algorithms.

Radar Subsystem (Orange):

  • Chest Movement Indicators: Monitors subtle chest movements to detect micro-motions associated with fatigue.
  • XE125 Radar Sensor Board: Gathers radar data to track chest movement patterns.
  • Radar Micro-Motion Monitoring: Analyzes radar data to identify irregularities indicating potential fatigue or unconsciousness.

Power (Yellow):

  • 12V Battery: Powers all system components, ensuring stable and continuous operation.

Processing Unit (Jetson Nano):

  • Jetson Nano: Serves as the central processing hub, integrating data from radar and camera subsystems to identify fatigue indicators and activate alerts.

Alert Subsystem (Green):

  • USB 3-Speed Vibration Motor: Activates physical vibration alerts to awaken the driver.
  • Active Piezo Electric Buzzer: Emits an audible alarm as a secondary alert mechanism.
  • Alert User: Executes the final alert mechanism using both sound and vibration feedback to ensure driver awareness.
